It's me Mario

Mario Club (also known as Super Mario Club, NCL Super Mario Club and Super Mario Club Debug Team) is a subsidiary of Nintendo, responsible for the debugging of many of their games. They are usually credited under Debug, Testing or Special Thanks in Nintendo game credits.
